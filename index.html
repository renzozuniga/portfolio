01 About

A bit about me

I'm Renzo, a software engineer from Lima, Peru. I hold a Bachelor's in Informatic Engineering from PUCP and I've spent the last 7+ years building things — from serverless APIs on AWS to responsive frontends with Angular and React.

I've worked remotely with teams across Chile, Uruguay, Ecuador, and the US, designing microservices, automating infrastructure, and shipping products that serve millions. I care about clean architecture, good DX, and systems that don't break at 3am.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me watching football, listening to music, or lost in a good video game. I believe the best engineers are curious people — and curiosity doesn't stop at code.
